Group Processes

We as a group are pleased with our efforts in Assignment 2 and feel that our strength lies in our good communication, positivity, determination and flexibility. We developed some useful tools and techniques that will be employed in the assignment 3 process too. These include using a working to-do-list to keep up to date with what tasks need to be completed and who is working on what and staying in touch with each other via our highly active WhatsApp group chat. We have learned from our previous assignment to not underestimate the task of converting our report to website form and will setup a GitHub repository and work on the website from an earlier stage.


Career Plans

Unsurprisingly from a group of IT students, we are all pursuing careers that require a certain level of technical expertise. A few differences are apparent too, namely that Sneha has chosen managerial/leadership roles. This is not surprising given her natural leadership skills as demonstrated by their career experience and personality test results.

While a lot of these positions may be performed remotely and may include elements of remote work, Jake was the only group member to choose a specifically remote position. Murray and Sneha were the only members to choose positions with a heavy focus on customer service.

Our specific career plans all involve further training and experience. Some common elements to our career plans include: continuing the Bachelor of IT to completion; learning specific tools, coding languages and techniques; further training and education including specific technical certificates and managerial certificates; and gaining industry experience through either relevant current employment, entry level IT jobs or internships.
