An undertaking of this size requires significant collaboration and cooperation between a large number of people with a vast array of different skills, both technical and non-technical in nature. We will need people with knowledge and expertise in the specific software, languages, visual and audio tools necessary for this project. We will need people on team who excel in creativity, innovation, teamwork, leadership, managerial and coordination skills.




Supposing a group of venture capitalists were to fund development of this project, we would assemble a team to take our project to the next phase. A suitable team would consist of a project manager, lead programmer, head of art/animation, sound engineer and writer. These 4 members of the team will all work under directions of the project manager and game designer and in turn will be in charge of their own teams and outsourcing tasks to assist in meeting objectives.

Project Manager

The project manager is responsible for developing and maintaining the project plan. The project plan will need to include the scope, scheduling, budget, risks, quality, human resources, procurement requirements, and document assumptions and decisions. The project manager must be informed on all elements of the project and is responsible for consulting with stakeholders and sponsors, as well as leading the members of the project team. The success of the project is dependent on the project manager facilitating the necessary outcomes required to reach completion.

    Skills required

    IT Specific

  • Unreal Engine 4/ C++
  • Understanding of animation processes
  • Experience working with other people's code
  • Project Management accreditations: PRINCE, Scrum Master, MSP, Agile

    • General

  • Exemplary communication skills
  • Team leadership skills
  • Experience with project planning and management

Lead Programmer

The lead programmer will need to have extensive experience in Unreal Engine 4 using C++. They must be proficient in writing reliable and efficient code, and in particular in coding gameplay features to completion.

    Skills required

    IT Specific

  • Familiarity with the C++ Language
  • Experience in software engineering from start to finish

    • General

  • Communication skills (both verbal and written)
  • Troubleshooting Experience
  • Strong decision making skills

Sound Engineer

The sound engineer will be responsible for all aspects of audio in the game including, soundtrack, sound effects and voices and therefore must have the following extensive experience: music production, sound design, recording, mixing, balancing and editing.

    Skills required

    IT/Audio Specific

  • Audio recording, design, processing and mixing skills
  • Experience with current audio engineering software (REAPER, Pro Tools, Logic, Live etc.)
  • Experience with audio hardware, such as recording interfaces, microphones and outboard processing.

    • General

  • Excellent communication and organisiational skills
  • Quality assurance and control
  • Detail Oriented

Writer

The writer will oversee bringing the vision of the game designer to the animators and programmers and must have strong story boarding and writing skills. They must be creative and have impeccable listening, comprehension and communication skills.

As team leaders, these individuals will spend a large portion of their time coordinating their teams, reporting to each other, and the dame designer. As such, they must all possess strong written and verbal communication skills and display exemplary teamwork and leadership.

    Skills required

    IT Specific

  • Experience with Unreal Engine 4
  • Expert knowledge in Microsoft Office 360
  • Microsoft Windows

    • General

  • Outstanding written and verbal skills
  • Exceptional proofreading and editing skills
  • Experience structuring multiple branching storylines
